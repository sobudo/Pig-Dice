Roll Piggy, Roll

A Game of Dice

Let's Roll...

You need 2 players. Choose a player to go first. Player 1 rolls the dice and scores as many points as the total shown in the box. If the player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points he accumulated that turn, and he passes the die to the next player. To To avoid rolling a 1 you can stop your turn at any time by clicking 'pause' (you will retain any points you have accumulated up to then) and letting Player 2 continue. The game passes from player to player until a winner is determined.
